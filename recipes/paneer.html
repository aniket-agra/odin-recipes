Kadhayi Paneer

Description

Kadhayi Paneer is a spicy, flavorful dish originating from north India. It is best enjoyed with naan or rice. It may be made dry or with gravy.

Ingredients

  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Green Chillies
  • Ginger Juliennes
  • Coriander leaves
  • Kasuri Methi
  • Kadhayi Paneer/Gosht Masala
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Oil

Steps

  1. In a kadai, heat 3 tablespoons oil. Add ⅓ to ½ cup finely chopped onions.
  2. Sauté onions on medium-low to medium heat until they soften and turn translucent.
  3. Then add 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste.
  4. Sauté for a few seconds till the raw aroma of ginger-garlic goes away.
  5. Now add 2.5 to 3 cups finely chopped tomatoes.
  6. Mix well and sauté tomatoes for 3 to 4 minutes on a medium-low to medium heat.
  7. Then add the kadai masala to the tomatoes.
  8. Mix well. Sauté the tomatoes till the whole mixture turns into a paste and starts separating from the oil.
  9. Now add ¾ to 1 cup capsicum julienne (thin slices).
  10. Sauté the capsicum for 3 to 4 minutes on medium-low to medium heat.
  11. Add 1 to 2 green chilies or 1 small serrano pepper (slit) and about ½ cup water.
  12. Mix very well and sauté until the capsicum is al dente.
  13. Once the capsicum is cooked to your liking, add garam masala powder and salt as required. Mix these with the rest of the masala.
  14. Now add the paneer cubes (250 grams).
  15. Lastly add 1 teaspoon crushed kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves), ginger julienne (from 1 inch ginger) and 2 tablespoons coriander leaves. Mix again and turn off the heat.
