Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is made from smashed avocados and is served widely as a side. It was first developed in Mexico and is popularly eaten with nachos, burritos, and goes well with most other Mexican food.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatos, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt.
  2. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic.
  3. Stir in cayenne pepper.
  4. Refrigerate for 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.
